##Planning a Graphic
A specification(SPEC) is a plan that is followed to create a graphic. Planning your graphic is important
for a few main reasons:
- It means you will follow all the rules for producing a graphic (Grammer of Graphs)
- Lets you see exactly what you need to do, so you can check of different tasks and make sure you dont forget anything.
- It allows for reproducability.If your SPEC is well written and concise, someone can recreate your graphic using just your specification. 

##what makes good graphic plan?
There are many aspects that makes a good graphic plan. These include data, aesthetics, geometrics, scales, coordinates and guides. These are also a part of the grammer of graphics.

The aesthetics are the design properties of the graphic. For example, aesthetics includes the x and y coordinates, and the colour you want your grpahic to
